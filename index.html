The Linux Foundation: Open Source Summit

June 21-24 · Austin, Texas & Virtual

Bringing Open Together

Overview

Open Source Summit is the premier event for open source developers, technologists, and community leaders to collaborate, share information, solve problems, and gain knowledge, furthering open source innovation and ensuring a sustainable open source ecosystem. It is the gathering place for open-source code and community contributors.

Open Source Summit is a conference umbrella, composed of a collection of events covering the most important technologies, topics, and issues affecting open source today.

Academic Registration

Academic registration is for current students and full-time faculty members. Students and full-time faculty will be required to upload a photo of their valid student/Faculty ID when registering for the conference.

Hobbyist Registration

You may choose this selection if you are paying for yourself to attend this event and are currently active in the community. Please contact us at events@linuxfoundation.org to request the Hobbyist Fee access code. Note, there are a limited number of Hobbyist access codes available and this discount may not be combined with any additional discounts.
